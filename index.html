About the Property

Where Kerala Tradition
Meets Forest Serenity

Nestled in the forested outskirts of Bangalore near Bannerghatta National Park, Forest Point Homes is a soulful retreat built in the timeless tradition of Kerala mud and stone architecture. Thick walls that breathe, natural materials, and open verandas frame a world where birdsong replaces traffic and starry skies replace city glow.

Just 35 km from Bangalore, the property spans three beautifully appointed row houses accommodating up to 30 guests — surrounded by forest trails, a shimmering Buddha lotus pond, a nearby waterfall, and the sounds of unspoiled nature.
